Why Coding?

Growing up, I never saw myself getting into programming or anything tech-related, but after some time out of work since the pandemic, I’ve considered a lot of options for career changes. I was looking for a few things. A job:

  • ...in an industry that is growing and will have a job for me.
  • ...which can give me the scope to find work anywhere.
  • ...where I can be always learning and improving my skills.
  • ...whose skills I can use in working on my hobbies (e.g. using programming knowledge to develop small video games)
  • ...that is intellectually stimulating and forces me to learn how to think in a completely different way
  • ...that offers a nice balance between working independently and working in a team.
